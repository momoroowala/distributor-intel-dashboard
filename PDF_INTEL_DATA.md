- $52,000 average revenue loss per commercial theft

- 75% of stolen equipment is NEVER recovered

### Criminal Tactics Against Distributors
- Scout locations in advance (shift changes, delivery schedules, security lapses)
- Most vulnerable: early mornings, nights, weekends, holidays
- Testing perimeter, exploiting blind spots, disabling security, using inside info, faking credentials
- Red flags: suspicious vehicles at odd hours, people taking photos, spikes in trespassing

### 10-Point Vulnerability Assessment
1. Local Crime Rate awareness
2. High-Value Asset protection
3. Perimeter Barriers & Fencing
4. Entry & Access Points (unsecured gates, shared codes)
5. Lighting & Visibility (dark corners, burned-out bulbs)
6. Surveillance & Monitoring
7. Landscaping & Environmental Design (CPTED principles)
8. Security Guards & Patrol Routes
9. Alarm & Intrusion Detection
10. Employee & Vendor Security Protocols

### Risk Levels
- Low Risk: "Fortified and Proactive" - multiple layers, 24/7, no blind spots
- Moderate Risk: "Defensible Yet Vulnerable" - some measures but gaps exist
- High Risk: "Targeted, High Exposure" - minimal security, easy target
